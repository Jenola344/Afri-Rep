Built for the African Context

Traditional trust systems meet modern blockchain technology.

Portable Reputation

Your 0-1000 Rep Score travels with you. Built through community vouches, it proves your reliability to employers and lenders across borders.

Cross-Border Trust Bridges

Our algorithm translates local trust into continental credibility. A vouch from Kenya helps you land a gig in Nigeria.

AfriDollar (AFD)

A pan-African stablecoin pegged 1:1 to USD. On-ramp via M-Pesa, MTN, or local bank transfers in 14+ African currencies.

Inner Circle DAOs

Digital adaptation of Ajo/Stokvel/Iqub. Form reputation-gated savings groups with transparent, on-chain treasury management.

How Afri Rep Works

Four simple steps to unlock your potential.

1

Claim Your Identity

Sign up in under 60 seconds. Verify your phone number and connect your preferred mobile money or local bank account.
